Booking and Payment

  • A 50% deposit is required at the time of booking to reserve your spot.
  • The remaining balance is due 14 days before your scheduled hike.
  • Bookings are confirmed only after the deposit has been received.
  • Group size is limited to 6 guests per expedition. Early booking is recommended.

Cancellation Policy

  • 30+ days before the hike: Full refund minus a $25 processing fee.
  • 14 - 29 days before the hike: 50% refund of the total trip cost.
  • Less than 14 days before the hike: No refund. You may transfer your booking to another person at no additional charge.
  • No-shows: No refund.
  • We encourage all guests to purchase trip insurance to protect against unexpected cancellations, travel delays, or other unforeseen circumstances.

Weather and Guide Cancellations

  • If Deliberate Pace Guiding cancels a hike due to severe weather, unsafe trail conditions, or other safety concerns, you will receive a full refund or the option to reschedule at no additional cost.
  • Light rain, overcast skies, and cool temperatures are normal conditions in both Appalachia and Southeast Alaska. We hike in these conditions and do not cancel for mild weather.
  • The guide reserves the right to modify the itinerary, change trails, or shorten a hike at any time based on conditions or group safety.

Assumption of Risk

Hiking and outdoor activities involve inherent risks including but not limited to:

  • Uneven, rocky, or slippery terrain
  • Wildlife encounters (bears, snakes, insects)
  • Rapidly changing weather conditions
  • Physical exertion and fatigue
  • Remote locations with limited access to emergency services

All guests are required to sign a liability waiver and assumption of risk agreement before participating in any expedition. By booking a hike, you acknowledge that you understand these risks and choose to participate voluntarily.

Health and Fitness

  • Guests are responsible for accurately representing their fitness level and any medical conditions when booking.
  • Please inform us of any allergies, medications, or health concerns that may affect your participation.
  • The guide reserves the right to exclude a participant from a hike if their condition poses a safety risk to themselves or the group.

Leave No Trace

  • All guests are expected to follow Leave No Trace principles.
  • Pack out everything you bring in.
  • Stay on designated trails.
  • Respect wildlife and other visitors.
  • Deliberate Pace Guiding reserves the right to end a hike early if a guest repeatedly disregards Leave No Trace practices or guide instructions.

Photography and Media

  • Deliberate Pace Guiding may take photos during hikes for use on the website, social media, and promotional materials.
  • If you prefer not to be photographed, please let us know before the hike begins.

Questions about our policies? Get in touch and we'll be happy to discuss.
